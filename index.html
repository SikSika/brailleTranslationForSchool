Our Services

1. Translating Books into Braille: We offer professional translation services to convert normal books into braille. Our team of skilled translators ensures accurate and high-quality translations, preserving the essence and meaning of the original text.

2. 3D Printing of Braille Books: Once the translation is complete, we utilize advanced 3D printing technology to create physical braille versions of the translated books. These tactile books allow visually impaired individuals to read and experience the content through touch.

About Us

At Creative Secondary School, we are a team of passionate students dedicated to making literature accessible to visually impaired individuals. Our mission is to bridge the gap between the sighted and visually impaired communities by providing high-quality braille translations and 3D-printed braille books. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to enjoy the magic of reading and explore the vast world of literature.
